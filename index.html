Front End Web Developer

A front-end developer is someone who works on software, like desktop or mobile websites, that users interact with. Specifically, a front-end developer spends time on the side of technology that the user touches and sees. The front end is also commonly referred to as the client side, or more broadly speaking, the user interface.
